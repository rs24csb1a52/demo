Architecture

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CST) is an architectural masterpiece featuring a grand facade adorned with intricate carvings, domes, turrets, and pointed arches. The station's design incorporates elements of Victorian Gothic Revival architecture, blended seamlessly with traditional Indian motifs. Spanning across a vast area, CST boasts a magnificent central dome, ornamental ironwork, and stained-glass windows, making it a fine example of 19th-century engineering and craftsmanship. The interiors include high vaulted ceilings, grand staircases, and spacious platforms that reflect the grandeur of a bygone era.
